Nexus Gaming Series

The Nexus Gaming Series is a North American based Heroes of the Storm free amateur competitive league. Community members form their own squad of players and do battle in fun and enjoyable pixel-to-pixel combat! These teams strive to become champions of their assigned Divisions based on their skill level.

NGS is open to all, so get involved! Enjoy one of the best Heroes of the Storm experiences available. Including organized play, balanced competition, casted matches, written articles and more!

By the players; for the players
