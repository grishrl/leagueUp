Regarding Pending Member Adds:

Flags for possible denial or further review:
  • Ideally, try to only approve adds in your assigned division.
  • Do not approve any adds in a division in which you are a player.
  • Not placed in SL
  • Few games uploaded on HP (less than 150)
  • Abnormally high WR, higher than 55% (especially when combined with low number of games)
  • Is the account flagged as a potential smurf?
  • Is the account a legal add for the division? (You'll have to get a screen shot of the rank from the player or captain)
  • In the case that an account is close to the SL limit, be sure to check match history for tanking to get under.
